Salient Features:
Along with the changing world, the need for an application to simplify finding a tutor became necessary. This app will help you finding tutors not only for the Academics but for anything you want to learn. The teacher's profile will help you knowing the teacher's per hour rate as well as the specialized subjects.
Find Tutors:
Tutors express their qualities, teaching charges and other related information via their profile. You can find them by name via search bar at the top-most on your HOME screen. Also a link named "FIND TUTOR" is there in side menu which moves you to the search page where you can find teachers by name or by subject.
Handle Proposals
When a student writes a post for tuition, a teacher can send a proposal against the post. The student receives the proposals from teacher and decides which one is the most competitive teacher. Just click on the sender's name and you are directed to his/her profile.rtr
Save Posts:
For teachers, there is an option named "SAVE POSTS". If they find any intersting post for sending proposal later. They can unsave it anytime.
